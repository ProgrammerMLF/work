Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Power Protection Services Limited offer?

We offer electrical and mechanical engineering consultation, the buying of condemed automobiles, and procurement of scrap metals including iron, aluminum, copper, and many more services companies.

How can I contact Power Protection Services Limited?

You can reach us via phone at +2348012345678 or email us at info@compny. Additionally, you may fill out our contact form on the website for specific inquiries or to request a quote. Our main office it's in Ibadan

Do you provide custom engineering solutions?

Yes, we specialize in tailored engineering solutions to meet the unique needs of each client. Whether for industrial or commercial applications, our team ensures that each project aligns with specific requirements and safety standards.

How does your scrap metal buying process work?

We buy bulk quantities of scrap metals, such as iron, aluminum, and copper. Pricing is based on current market rates, and we offer competitive rates for larger quantities. Contact us for a quote and further details.

Do you sell scrap metals to individuals or only to recycling companies?

Our scrap metal sales primarily focus on large recycling companies. However, we may accommodate smaller quantities for commercial clients upon request. Please contact us to discuss your specific needs.

What areas do you serve?

Ibadan, but we serve clients nationwide for engineering consulting and scrap metal procurement. For large projects, our team may be available for on-site consultations and support depending on project scope and location.
